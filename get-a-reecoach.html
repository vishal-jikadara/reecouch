Who is a ReeCoach?

A professional qualified Dietitian / Clinical Nutritionist / Nutritionist who specializes in preventing and managing a health condition with dietary intervention and lifestyle counseling.

What does a ReeCoach do?

  • Analyse your body composition

  • Assess your health and lifestyle parameters

  • Review your medical, diet and lifestyle history

  • Understand your likes and dislikes

  • Help you achieve your health goals

  • Support and guide your progress

  • Plan a personalized nutrition plan

Why a ReeCoach?

Qualified dietitians have 3-5 years of formal education in Nutrition and Dietetics.

They use evidence-based scientifically validated information.

They tailor advice to your specific health needs and provide complete health support.

They adhere to ethical standards & regulations.
