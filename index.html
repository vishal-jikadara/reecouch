naaznin husein

co - founder and ceo

www.linkedin.com/in.

Naaznin Husein is a Co-founder of Reework, Shamrock Nutrascience, a pioneering digital nutrition app and software company. She holds esteemed positions such as Chairperson for Dietetics at Nutrify Today, a distinguished Founder Director at Freedom Wellness Management and is a key figure on the National Executive Committee of the Indian Dietetic Association.

Her roles include serving as a Nutritional Advisor for the Indian Navy's INS Hamla and providing expert consultation to prominent nutraceutical companies and food corporates. Naaznin’s credentials are notable, including certifications as a Diabetes Educator, FODMAP Nutritionist, Nutrigenomics Counsellor, and Bariatric Dietitian.

Her current research focuses on advancing preventive nutrition and its digital health applications. A prolific author, she has written for the Indian Navy, FSSAI, and The Yoga Institute. Naaznin is also a recognized media personality, frequently contributing to newspapers, TV shows, and radio. Her community involvement spans environmental activism and leadership roles in initiatives such as the Equal Streets Movement and Mumbai Car Free Day.
