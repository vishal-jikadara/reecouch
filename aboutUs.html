About Reework

An integrated Mind and Body digital platform that helps achieve your health goal with the guidance of a qualified Dietitian near you.

Reework works with you, for you and through you. We respond, we do not dictate. Big changes to your life need not make loud announcements. The real shift we bring about is within you.

Using human wisdom and health tech, we catalyse a permanent shift in how you perceive self care. This new form of self love is built for people who want to live, not exist. Reework is beyond being a fitness tracker. We are your new measure of holistic living.

Our vision

Democratising Personal Nutrition

To create a robust network of nutritionists adept at maximising health tech, to simplify nutrition and help eradicate lifestyle induced diseases.

Our Mission

Humanising health data to shape and shift habits and nutrition.

To create a community that practises healthy personal rituals as a form of self love. Reework aims to ingrain and improve healthy habits through precision nutrition.
